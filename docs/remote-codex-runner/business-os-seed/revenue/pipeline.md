## Why We Reach Out To These People

Outreach is aimed at buyers with immediate operational pain and authority to buy quickly.

1. AI founders: they need execution systems now and can approve quickly.
2. Agencies and consultancies: they need repeatable delivery operations for clients.
3. Solo operators: they need automation and follow-through, not more tools.

This is why these segments are prioritized: short sales cycle plus visible value in 48h-5d.

## Qualification Rules

A lead is qualified only if all are true:

- direct response from a real person
- states an active execution bottleneck
- agrees to discuss scope/timeline
