# Outside Bazaar Scorecard

## Purpose

Compare the current Bazaar path against three outside-Bazaar revenue angles for the `"$1,000"` net revenue goal by `2026-03-31`.

## Current Bazaar Path

- **Path:** Bazaar / x402 seller route
- **Current lead wedge:** `restricted-party-screen`
- **Bundle / upsell:** `vendor-entity-brief`
- **Primary advantage:** already has structure, live artifacts, and a clear payment rail
- **Primary risk:** crowded market bands, weak telemetry trust, and buyer sensitivity to price

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Outside-Bazaar angle 1: direct workflow service sold to a specific buyer | Fast if there is an existing warm buyer list or an immediate pain point | High, because the buyer can see the human / operator value | Mid to high, because it can be sold as a workflow outcome, not a raw endpoint | Medium to high, because it needs outreach and custom fit | Strong if one or two buyers can close quickly; good bridge if Bazaar demand is weak |

## Readout

### Best Speed to Cash

- The current Bazaar path is still the fastest if it converts without heroic pricing.
- If Bazaar remains noisy, a direct workflow service is the next fastest path because it can be sold to a real buyer without waiting on marketplace discovery.

### Best Credibility

- The direct workflow service and the productized compliance package are the most credible outside-Bazaar plays.
- They map to clear buyer pain and do not depend on broad marketplace attention.

### Best Fit For The EOM Target

- Best fit overall: current Bazaar path if the offer pack, pricing, and telemetry become believable enough to generate real probes.
- Best fallback: direct workflow service, because it can close a small number of higher-value buyers faster than a low-visibility marketplace route.
- Weakest fit: research brief, unless it becomes a lead generator for the stronger offers.

## Working Assumption

- Keep Bazaar as the lead because it already exists.
- If the current lead wedge does not produce non-self probes and paid calls soon, shift energy to a direct workflow offer instead of adding more Bazaar routes.
- Avoid any angle that needs a long build before it can ask for money.

This week counts as progress if at least one of these happens:

- a clearer direct buyer offer is written down
- a live probe or paid call appears on the current Bazaar path
- a credible outside-Bazaar buyer list or outreach path is identified
- the current pathway is ruled out honestly instead of being assumed to work
