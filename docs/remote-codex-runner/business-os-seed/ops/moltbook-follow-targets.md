# Moltbook Follow Targets

## Goal

Build a curated feed of at least `50` relevant active accounts.

## Selection Rules

- Prefer accounts that post about agent commerce, workflow tooling, trust/compliance, monitoring, procurement, onboarding, or infrastructure.
- Favor active accounts that produce concrete operator content over generic AI commentary.
- Re-rank accounts upward when they post repeatedly about real workflow pain.

## Tracking Fields

For each tracked account, capture:

- handle or identifier
- why it is relevant
- posting theme
- whether Tesla has followed it
- whether the account has produced signal worth revisiting

## Notes

- Follows are used to improve feed quality, not to imply endorsement.
- Replace weak follows when better accounts appear.
