# Official Business Plan (No API Route)

Date: `2026-03-19`  
Mission: generate at least `$1,000` net revenue by `2026-03-31`.

## 1) Executive Summary

This is a service business sprint. Endpoint/API monetization is removed from this plan.

- Primary offer: `Revenue Ops Build Week` (`$1,200`)
- Fast-entry offer: `Agent OS Setup Sprint` (`$600`)
- Expansion offer: `Managed Agent Ops Retainer` (`$400/mo`)
- Success condition: at least one `$1,000+` paid close or two `$600` closes before EOM

## 2) Buyer Strategy

### Who we sell to

1. AI founders with execution bottlenecks
2. agencies/consultancies that need repeatable delivery systems
3. solo operators who need structure, follow-through, and automation

### Why these buyers

- pain is urgent and visible
- short decision cycles
- value can be delivered within 48h to 5 business days

## 3) Offer Stack

| Offer | Price | Delivery Window | What Buyer Gets |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Agent OS Setup Sprint | `$600` fixed | 48 hours | installed and tuned multi-agent operating setup for one function |
| Revenue Ops Build Week | `$1,200` fixed | 5 business days | working outreach + follow-up + proof cadence with clear ownership |
| Managed Agent Ops Retainer | `$400/mo` | ongoing | daily lane operation and weekly operating proof |

## 4) Revenue Math

### Net target math

- Net target: `$1,000`
- Estimated delivery/tool costs: `~$80`
- Gross needed: `~$1,080`

### Base-case path

- `1 x Revenue Ops Build Week ($1,200)`  
- Estimated net: `~$1,120`

### Backup path

- `2 x Agent OS Setup Sprint ($600) = $1,200 gross`

## 5) Outreach Plan

### Why outreach exists

Outreach is used to get paid service conversations, not product traffic.

### Outreach objectives by EOM

- 40 qualified targets
- 8 live conversations
- 4 scoped opportunities
- 1 close at `$1,000+` or 2 closes at `$600`

### Core CTA

- `If I build your execution system in 5 business days with fixed scope and fixed price, do you want the scope now?`

## 6) Daily Operating Loop

Weekday lane cadence:

- Tesla `10:15` signal capture for founder/operator pain
- Apollo `11:00` direct outreach and follow-up
- Carver `13:15` offer/pricing and objections
- Franklin `13:45` channel and close-speed optimization
- Proof-check `16:45` verified evidence publish

Daily steps:

1. send targeted outreach for paid service offers
2. convert reply into a scope discussion
3. send fixed scope + fixed price
4. close paid work
5. publish verified proof

## 7) KPIs

- qualified responses
- scopes sent
- paid closes
- closed gross dollars
- follow-ups completed

## 8) Risk Controls

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| no qualified responses | no pipeline | rotate segment and tighten message quickly |
| replies but no scope acceptance | stalled closes | simplify scope and reduce buyer effort |
| underpriced work | misses net target | enforce fixed scope and minimum pricing |
| execution drift | no close proof | daily proof-check with evidence-only logging |

## 9) Official Statement

This operating cycle is a service-only revenue sprint. Any endpoint/API paid-route strategy is out of scope until after the `$1,000` net target is achieved.
